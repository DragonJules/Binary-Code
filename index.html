History

I Ching

  • 9th century BC
  • One of the oldest binary system
  • Based on the duality of yin and yang

Gottfried Leibniz

  • 17th century
  • Inventor of the modern binary number system

George Boole

  • 19th century
  • Author of "The mathematics analysis of Logic" (1847)
  • Inventor of Boolean algebra

Claude Shannon

  • 20th century
  • Made the links between Boolean algebra and electric circuits
  • Enabled the use of binary code in many important fields
